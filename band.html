Sanam

SANAM is a popular Mumbai-based Indian pop-rock band, widely known for their soulful renditions of classic Bollywood hits and original music. Formed in 2010 (originally as the SQS Project), the quartet has become one of India's biggest music artists on digital platforms, with a massive following on YouTube.


SANAM is celebrated for creating music that appeals across generations, with a goal of spreading love, positivity, and unity through their work. They are known for giving a fresh, contemporary pop-rock sound to old classics while maintaining the "soul" of the original song, which has helped bring families together through shared musical experiences. The band has performed globally and was featured on the cover of Rolling Stone Magazine in December 2018.
